Apple Watch SE 3 Launched Today: Budget-Friendly with Always-On Display & S10 Chip

Apple revealed the Apple Watch SE 3, the updated budget model featuring significant new enhancements including always-on display, S10 chip, and advanced health tracking capabilities.

Apple revealed the Apple Watch SE 3, the updated budget model featuring significant new enhancements that bring premium features to the affordable smartwatch segment. This latest iteration combines the best of Apple's technology with an accessible price point, making advanced health tracking and smartwatch functionality available to a broader audience.

New Chip & Always-On Display

The Apple Watch SE 3 is powered by the new S10 processor, delivering enhanced performance and efficiency compared to previous SE models. This powerful chip enables smoother animations, faster app launches, and improved overall responsiveness throughout the user interface.

One of the most significant upgrades is the addition of an always-on display, a feature previously reserved for the more expensive Apple Watch Series models. This means you can now see the time, date, and important information at a glance without needing to raise your wrist or tap the screen.

Advanced Health & Gesture Features

The Apple Watch SE 3 introduces several new health monitoring capabilities, including sleep apnea detection and retrospective ovulation estimates. These features provide valuable insights into your health and wellness, helping you make informed decisions about your lifestyle and habits.

The watch also includes a temperature sensor that can track changes in your body temperature over time, providing additional data for health monitoring and fitness tracking. This sensor works in conjunction with other health features to provide a comprehensive picture of your well-being.

New gesture controls include wrist-flick and double-tap functionality, allowing you to interact with your watch without touching the screen. These intuitive gestures make it easier to use your watch during workouts or when your hands are occupied.

Faster Charging & 5G Connectivity

The Apple Watch SE 3 features 2x faster charging compared to previous models, getting you back to full power in half the time. This improvement makes it more convenient to keep your watch charged and ready for use throughout the day.

Enhanced speaker audio provides clearer sound for calls, notifications, and Siri interactions. The improved audio quality makes it easier to use your watch for communication and voice commands, even in noisy environments.

Cellular support via 5G connectivity allows you to stay connected even when your iPhone isn't nearby. This feature is particularly useful for workouts, runs, or any time you want to leave your phone behind while staying connected.

Durability & Battery Life

The Apple Watch SE 3 is available in aluminum casing with midnight and starlight color options, providing a sleek and modern appearance. The Ion-X glass display offers excellent scratch resistance while maintaining clarity and touch sensitivity.

With IP-rated water resistance, the watch can handle swimming, workouts, and everyday activities without worry. The improved battery life provides up to 18 hours of usage on a single charge, ensuring your watch stays powered throughout your day.

Pricing and Availability

The Apple Watch SE 3 starts at $249 for the 40mm model, making it an excellent value proposition in the smartwatch market. Preorders are available now, with shipping beginning on September 19, coinciding with the release of watchOS 26.

For Indian customers, the Apple Watch SE 3 is expected to retail for approximately ₹20,700, making it an attractive option in the growing wearables market. The device will be available through Apple's official channels and authorized retailers across the country.

watchOS 26 & Liquid Glass UI

The Apple Watch SE 3 comes bundled with watchOS 26 and the new Liquid Glass UI, providing a fresh and intuitive user experience. The updated operating system includes new watch faces, improved health tracking features, and enhanced customization options.

The Liquid Glass UI introduces new visual elements and animations that make the interface feel more fluid and responsive. These design improvements work in harmony with the S10 chip to deliver a smooth and enjoyable user experience.

Why the Apple Watch SE 3 Matters

The Apple Watch SE 3 represents excellent value for money, delivering many premium features at an accessible price point. For users seeking comprehensive health tracking and smartwatch functionality without the premium price tag, the SE 3 offers the perfect balance.

In India's growing wearables market, the Apple Watch SE 3 provides an attractive entry point into the Apple ecosystem. Its combination of health tracking, connectivity, and smartwatch features makes it ideal for fitness enthusiasts, health-conscious individuals, and anyone looking to enhance their daily routine with smart technology.

Technical Specifications

  • Price: Starting at $249 (₹20,700 approx.)
  • Processor: S10 chip for enhanced performance
  • Display: Always-on display capability
  • Health Features: Sleep apnea detection, temperature sensor
  • Gestures: Wrist-flick and double-tap controls
  • Charging: 2x faster than previous models
  • Connectivity: 5G cellular support
  • Water Resistance: IP-rated for swimming and workouts
  • Battery Life: Up to 18 hours
  • Availability: Preorder now, ships September 19

Comparison with Previous SE Models

Compared to the Apple Watch SE 2, the new model offers significant improvements in performance, display technology, and health tracking capabilities. The always-on display alone makes it feel much more premium, while the S10 chip ensures smooth operation of all features.

The addition of advanced health features like sleep apnea detection and temperature monitoring brings the SE 3 closer to the capabilities of the more expensive Series models, making it an excellent choice for users who want comprehensive health tracking without the premium price.

Target Audience

The Apple Watch SE 3 is perfect for first-time smartwatch buyers, fitness enthusiasts on a budget, and users who want essential Apple Watch features without the premium price tag. Its combination of health tracking, connectivity, and smartwatch functionality makes it suitable for a wide range of users and use cases.

Whether you're looking to track your fitness goals, monitor your health, or simply stay connected throughout the day, the Apple Watch SE 3 provides all the essential features you need in an affordable and accessible package.

Conclusion

The Apple Watch SE 3 successfully bridges the gap between budget-friendly smartwatches and premium Apple Watch models. With its always-on display, advanced health tracking, and improved performance, it offers exceptional value for money and makes advanced smartwatch technology accessible to a broader audience.

For users in India and around the world who want to experience the benefits of an Apple Watch without the premium price tag, the SE 3 represents an excellent choice that delivers on both functionality and value.
